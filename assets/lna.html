LNA measurements

Custom cavity low-noise amplifiers with bandpass filters, procured April 2026.

Four custom cavity LNAs with integrated bandpass filters were built by Tommy Henderson (WD5AGO) specifically for the SALSA L-band receivers. Each unit consists of a cavity LNA followed by a cavity bandpass filter. Power is supplied via a Bias-T placed between the filter output and the LNA — the cavity filter does not pass DC, so the Bias-T cannot be placed on the antenna side of the filter.

Specifications

Recommended operating range: 1350–1600 MHz

Power supply: 11–20 VDC, ~145 mA (±10 mA)

Unit variation

Three of the four units measure approximately 27 ±1 dB gain at 1420 MHz. The fourth unit has 25 dB gain at 1420 MHz — it was intentionally tuned with a wider bandwidth toward 1575 MHz, giving a flatter response across the band. All four units are better than −40 dB above 2 GHz.
